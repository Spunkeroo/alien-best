1. Anker Soundcore Space One -- Best Overall ($80)

The Anker Soundcore Space One delivers the best overall package under $100 in 2026. Anker has quietly become one of the most competent audio brands in the budget space, and the Space One represents their most refined over-ear offering. The ANC is effective across a wide frequency range, the sound profile is balanced and enjoyable, and the 40-hour battery life with ANC enabled is exceptional.

Noise Canceling

The Space One uses a hybrid ANC system with feedforward and feedback microphones that measures and counters ambient noise in real time. In testing, it reduced office noise by approximately 80 percent, subway rumble by 75 percent, and airplane engine drone by 70 percent. These figures are 15 to 20 percent below the Sony WH-1000XM6 ($350) but represent outstanding performance for the price. The transparency mode is also excellent, allowing natural conversation without removing the headphones.

Sound Quality

The 40mm drivers produce a balanced sound signature with a slight emphasis on bass that adds warmth to music without muddying the midrange. The Soundcore app allows full EQ customization with a 10-band equalizer. Hi-Res Audio support via LDAC codec is included, which is rare at this price and delivers noticeably better wireless audio quality than standard SBC or AAC codecs.

Comfort and Build

Memory foam ear cushions with protein leather covering create a comfortable seal. At 260 grams, the Space One is lighter than most over-ear headphones, making it comfortable for 4 to 6 hour sessions. Foldable design for portability. Build quality is good for the price with no creaks or flex.

2. JBL Tune 770NC -- Best Value ($70)

JBL's Tune 770NC is the best choice for bass lovers on a budget. JBL has always been known for punchy, energetic bass, and the 770NC delivers that signature sound with competent noise canceling at an aggressive $70 price point.

Sound Profile

The JBL Tune 770NC has a bass-forward sound signature that adds impact to hip-hop, electronic, pop, and rock music. Bass extends deep without excessive bloat, and the midrange remains clear enough for vocals to cut through. The treble is slightly rolled off, reducing harshness but also reducing detail in the highest frequencies. For casual listening, the sound is engaging and fun.

Battery and Features

Battery life is 44 hours with ANC on, which is outstanding. A 5-minute quick charge provides 3 hours of playback. Multipoint connection allows pairing with two devices simultaneously. The JBL Headphones app provides EQ adjustment and ANC level control.

3. Sony WH-CH720N -- Best ANC ($98)

Sony brings its class-leading noise canceling expertise to the budget segment. While the ANC does not match the flagship WH-1000XM6, it benefits from Sony's years of ANC algorithm development and is noticeably more effective than most competitors at this price. If ANC performance is your top priority under $100, this is the headphone to buy.

ANC Performance

Sony's Integrated Processor V1 brings processing power from the premium lineup. The result is ANC that adapts to your environment in real time, handling sudden noise changes smoothly. Low-frequency noise reduction is particularly strong, making these excellent for airplane and train travel.

Sound and Features

Sony's DSEE upscales compressed audio files, making streaming music sound noticeably better. The sound signature is balanced and slightly warm. Battery life is 35 hours with ANC. At 192 grams, these are the lightest headphones in the comparison, ideal for extended wear.

4. 1MORE SonoFlow SE -- Best Budget Pick ($60)

The 1MORE SonoFlow SE proves that good ANC is available even at $60. This is the headphone for shoppers on the tightest budget who still want active noise canceling that actually works.

What You Get for $60

Hybrid ANC with three modes, 50-hour battery life with ANC, Bluetooth 5.3, Hi-Res Audio support via LDAC, comfortable over-ear design, and a carrying case. ANC effectiveness is about 65 to 70 percent of the Soundcore Space One, which at $20 less represents a fair trade-off. Sound quality is warm and pleasant.

5. Edifier W820NB Plus -- Most Comfortable ($65)

Edifier focuses on comfort with extra-thick memory foam ear pads, a lightly padded headband, and a featherweight 220-gram build. For all-day wear at a desk or during travel, the W820NB Plus is the most comfortable option under $100. ANC is good but not class-leading, and the sound signature is neutral and accurate, suitable for podcasts, audiobooks, and music equally.

All-Day Comfort

The oversized ear pads fully enclose the ear with minimal clamping force. The headband distributes pressure evenly. Multiple reviewers report wearing these for 6 to 8 hours without discomfort, which is rare at any price. Battery life is 49 hours with ANC, among the longest on this list.

6. Sennheiser Accentum SE -- Best Sound ($99)

Sennheiser's reputation for audio excellence extends to the Accentum SE, which delivers the best pure sound quality of any headphone under $100. The 37mm transducers produce a wide, detailed soundstage with accurate instrument separation, controlled bass, clear midrange, and extended treble. Audiophiles who prioritize sound quality over ANC performance should start here.

Audio Excellence

Where most budget headphones sacrifice treble clarity or muddy the bass, Sennheiser maintains its signature balance. Acoustic music, classical, jazz, and vocal-heavy tracks sound particularly impressive. The ANC is competent but ranks behind Sony and Anker in raw noise reduction. The trade-off is intentional: Sennheiser prioritizes audio fidelity over ANC aggression.

What Matters Most in Budget ANC Headphones

ANC Effectiveness

Not all ANC is created equal. Budget ANC typically reduces ambient noise by 15 to 25 decibels versus 25 to 35 decibels for flagship models. The practical difference is noticeable in very loud environments but minimal in moderately noisy settings like offices and coffee shops. For most everyday use, budget ANC is more than sufficient.

Comfort

If you wear headphones for hours daily, comfort matters more than ANC specs. Pay attention to ear pad material, clamping force, weight (under 270g ideal), and headband padding. The Edifier W820NB Plus leads here, but all models on this list are comfortable for 2 to 4 hour sessions minimum.

Battery Life

Every headphone on this list offers 35 to 50 hours of battery life with ANC enabled. At these numbers, battery life is effectively a non-issue for daily use. All models support quick charging.

Sound Quality

Budget headphones have improved dramatically. The weakest link is usually treble, which can sound harsh in cheaper models. If sound quality is your priority, the Sennheiser Accentum SE is the clear winner. For most listeners, any headphone on this list provides enjoyable sound.

Build Quality

Budget headphones use plastic construction, which keeps weight down but reduces durability compared to metal-reinforced flagships. The hinges and folding mechanisms are the most common failure points. Handle with reasonable care and expect 2 to 4 years of reliable use from any model here.

Wait for Sales

Headphones are among the most frequently discounted electronics. Amazon Prime Day (July), Black Friday (November), and back-to-school sales (August) regularly drop prices 20 to 40 percent. The $80 Soundcore Space One frequently hits $55 to $60 during sales. Patience saves real money.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you get good noise canceling headphones under $100?

Best overall: Anker Soundcore Space One ($80). Best value: JBL Tune 770NC ($70). Best ANC: Sony WH-CH720N ($98). Best budget: 1MORE SonoFlow SE ($60). Best sound: Sennheiser Accentum SE ($99).

Are budget ANC headphones worth it for airplane travel?

Yes. They reduce engine drone by 60 to 80 percent. Budget ANC excels at low-frequency constant noise. Premium headphones are better in very loud environments, but budget ANC is absolutely worth using for occasional travel.

How long do budget ANC headphones last?

Battery life ranges from 30 to 60 hours with ANC. Physical durability varies, with Anker and Sony models typically lasting 2 to 4 years. Build materials are primarily plastic but well-assembled.
