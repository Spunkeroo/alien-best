Where to Stargaze in 2026

Gear is only half the equation. Location matters enormously for both stargazing and UFO spotting. The best sites share a few characteristics: minimal light pollution, clear horizons, high altitude, and dry climate. National parks, designated dark sky preserves, and remote desert locations consistently produce the best sightings.

The same locations that produce the best stargazing also tend to be hotspots for UAP sightings. This is not a coincidence -- clearer skies reveal more of what is actually up there.
